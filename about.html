About Me

Founder, builder, and student in Austin, TX.

What I'm Building

I'm the Founder and CEO of Impact Margin, a sustainability platform that helps businesses cut costs and operate more efficiently by making going green a growth strategy, not a tradeoff. We're building software that streamlines sustainable operations for businesses, along with a paperless receipt hardware device that plugs into legacy and modern point of sale systems where a thermal printer would normally connect, eliminating paper costs and reducing waste.

Impact Margin is a Delaware C-Corp. I work alongside a co-founder and CTO to develop the product and go to market. We're currently launching our products and executing a sales strategy to scale the business.

Background

I've been starting businesses since high school. Some failed fast, some made real money, and all of them taught me something. I built and operated an Ethereum mining rig, sourced products from manufacturers overseas through Alibaba for an athletic clothing brand and a pickleball brand, and launched consumer products before I could drive.

Before Impact Margin, I founded Red Bison Ventures, an early-stage entrepreneurial initiative focused on testing and launching scalable businesses. I also worked as a Legal Assistant at Willi Law Firm, P.C. in Austin for nearly five years, where I managed government filings, facilitated compliance work, collaborated with attorneys on trust instruments, and served as an authorized signing witness for legal documents.

Education

I'm currently a sophomore at Indiana University Bloomington, studying Merchandising and Product Development at the Eskenazi School with a 4.0 GPA. I'm pursuing a Business Minor and Real Estate Certificate through the Kelley School of Business. I'm a member of Phi Eta Sigma National Honor Society, the IU Innovates Entrepreneur Bootcamp, and the Retail Studies Organization.

Before IU, I graduated from St. Andrew's Episcopal School, where I played Varsity Football and Varsity Golf and founded the Investing Club, recruiting and training 31 members as president. I also attended Columbia University's National Student Leadership Conference for Business and Entrepreneurship.

Interests

I'm passionate about venture capital, real estate, and the intersection of technology and business growth. I taught myself to underwrite multifamily properties and published a 12-unit multifamily underwriting analysis on a real property near Indiana University, modeling in-place performance against a stabilized value-add scenario. I earned my Eagle Scout rank through Scouting America, where I served as Senior Patrol Leader.
